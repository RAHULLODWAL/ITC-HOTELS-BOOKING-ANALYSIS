# ITC-HOTELS-BOOKING-ANALYSIS

ITC Hotels: Revenue & Operations Analysis
A comprehensive data-driven analysis of ITC Hotels' financial performance, occupancy trends, and booking insights. This project evaluates key metrics to provide strategic recommendations for revenue growth and operational efficiency.

📊 Project Overview
This repository contains an analysis of ITC Hotels' performance (May–July 2022), focusing on revenue generation across properties, room classes, and booking platforms.

🔑 Key Insights
Revenue Performance: Generated ₹2.01 billion in total revenue with a 50.29% MoM growth.
Top Performer: Elite Rooms contributed the highest revenue (₹658.95M, ~32.8%), while Mumbai emerged as the leading city (₹0.56bn).
Occupancy Trends: Overall occupancy rate stood at 43.50%, with peaks highly correlated to weekend demand.
Cancellations: A 24.83% cancellation rate resulted in ₹298.77M in lost revenue, primarily driven by short-notice (0–5 days) bookings.

🛠️ Metrics Analyzed
RevPAR: ₹8.63K (Revenue per Available Room)
ADR: ₹14.92K (Average Daily Rate)
ALOS: 2.37 (Average Length of Stay)
Lead Time Analysis: Correlation between booking windows and cancellation risk.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations
Revenue Growth: Double down on Elite/Premium room marketing in Tier-1 cities.
Dynamic Pricing: Implement segment-specific offers for business weekdays and leisure weekends.
Cancellation Policy: Stricter policies for bookings made within a 5-day check-in window to minimize lost revenue.
